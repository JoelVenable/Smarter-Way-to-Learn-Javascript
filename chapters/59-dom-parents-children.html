Every node of a document is a parent and/or a child of another node. The 'document' node has no parent and itself is the parent of the 'HTML' node, and text content of a 'p' tag often have no children. xth child - In our simplified HTML document from the last lesson, 'head' is the first child of HTML, and 'body' is the 2nd child. Therefore 'head' and 'body' would be considered siblings.
